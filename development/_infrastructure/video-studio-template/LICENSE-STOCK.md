## Sourcing Instructions

When replacing placeholder audio files:
1. Visit a CC0/royalty-free source (Pixabay, Mixkit, Freesound CC0)
2. Search for the appropriate style (upbeat, ambient, etc.)
3. Download as MP3
4. Normalize audio levels (target -14 LUFS for background music)
5. Trim to 30-60 seconds for music, < 2 seconds for SFX
