## Why It Works

This post works because I open with a universal pain point, transition into actionable steps through simple bullet points, then I elevate the message by reframing past struggles as future strength. The short, punchy lines mirror how people actually process emotional content, making each point land with more impact.

## Template

[OBSTACLE] doesn't define your future.
The best [PROFESSION]?
They know how to turn [NEGATIVE] into [POSITIVE].
You can't control [EXTERNAL FACTOR].
But you can control:
- How you [ACTION1] from it
- How you [ACTION2] during it
- How you [ACTION3] through it
The hardest work isn't running from your [OBSTACLE].
It's choosing to face it [BRAVE DESCRIPTOR].
And using it to help others who are where you once were.
That's what separates [BASIC] from [EXCEPTIONAL].
That's what turns [CHALLENGE] into [TRIUMPH].
Want to turn your experience into a thriving [BUSINESS TYPE]?
[YOUR CALL-TO-ACTION]

## Example

Here's an example using the template, written from the perspective of a personal trainer:

Your weight doesn't define your future.
The best athletes?
They know how to turn failures into motivation.
You can't control your genetics.
But you can control:
- How you learn from it
- How you work through it
- How your fitness reflects it
The hardest work isn't running from your setbacks.
It's choosing to face it in the gym.
And using it to help others who are where you once were.
That's what separates giving up from breaking through.
That's what turns weakness into power.
Want to turn your experience into a thriving fitness journey?
I recorded 10+ hours of workout fundamentals to show you how.
It's called The Transformation Blueprint.
