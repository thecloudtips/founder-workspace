## Why It Works

This uses strong language in the opener: "nobody owns you." The Tweet then uses repetition to create a crescendo: NO investors, NO board, NO boss, and NO banks — hitting the reader like a combo punch. Then comes the flip, bringing in the downside, but it's secretly an upside that challenges the reader.

## Template

The biggest upside of [THING] is that [NO NEGATIVE THING]
No [BAD THING 1], no [BAD THING 2], no [BAD THING 3], no [BAD THING 4].
The downside is that if you fail, you're [FAUX NEGATIVE].
If you can accept that, you should [RELEVANT ACTION].
