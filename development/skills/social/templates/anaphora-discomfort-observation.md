## Why It Works

This specific post worked well because it offers a counterintuitive insight about life that resonates with lots of people. Short, punchy sentences and repetition ("is uncomfortable") for emphasis are used throughout.

## Template

A simple observation about [broad topic]:
It pays to embrace [challenging aspect] early on.
You'll be much better prepared than if you're always protected from it.
[Specific area of life] is [challenging aspect]. [Related area] is [challenging aspect]. [Broader category] is [challenging aspect].
When you've worked through [challenging aspect] and know that enemy well?
The [metaphor for challenge] becomes a lot easier.

## Example

A simple observation about language learning:
It pays to embrace mistakes early on.
You'll be much better prepared than if you're always protected from them.
Speaking a new language is uncomfortable. Writing in a foreign script is uncomfortable. Cultural immersion is uncomfortable.
When you've worked through embarrassment and know that enemy well?
The journey becomes a lot easier.
