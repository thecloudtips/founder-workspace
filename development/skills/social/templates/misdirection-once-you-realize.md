## Why It Works

This one is pretty simple. It starts by tapping into an idea that 1) nobody talks about and 2) is definitely true. That gets people to lean in. Then it challenges people to join the crowd once they've accepted that.

## Template

Once you realize [thing nobody talks about] you almost have no choice but to [join the crowd].

## Example

Once you realize lifting weights is ultimate fat burner you almost have no choice but to lift heavy every morning.
