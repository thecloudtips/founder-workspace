## Why It Works

This works for a few reasons:
1. The image stands out with the bold headline
2. It overcomes the idea that all startup founders have to be college kids or young people
3. It gets people to engage based on whether or not they believe it.

## Template

A study of [group]:
The average [characteristic] of [group] who [achieved something] is [surprising number/statistic].
Everyone's in a rush to [common goal], but shouldn't be.

## Example

A study of 1.5 million gym-goers:
The average age of people who achieved their body fat goal is 39.
Everyone's in a rush to get six-pack abs, but maybe they shouldn't be?
