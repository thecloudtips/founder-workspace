## Why It Works

This template taps into the emotional connection that comes from a common experience — going against the advice of skeptics to pursue a challenging goal. It validates those who have taken that path and resonates broadly because most people have faced similar pushback at some point.

## Template

Shout out to all the people who had enough courage to [Pursue a Challenging Goal] against the advice of their [Skeptical or Concerned Group].

## Example

Shout out to all the people who had enough courage to write and publish their own book against the advice of their very skeptical friends.
