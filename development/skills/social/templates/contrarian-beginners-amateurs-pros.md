## Why It Works

The contrast between beginners, amateurs, and pros usually works if you actually make it interesting or unique enough. It can't just be that each one is slightly better. The last one has to be surprising and thought-provoking.

## Template

Beginners: I got [NormalDecentThing]
Amateurs: I got [NormalBetterThing]
Pros: I got [SmallerNumberBiggerImpactThing]

It's about [ThingThatMatters].
Not [DumbThingThatDoesnt].
