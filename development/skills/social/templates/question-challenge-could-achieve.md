## Why It Works

This post works because I'm challenging the reader. I make it sound so easy ("talking about one thing they do exceptionally well on the internet every day for a few years") but the reality hits the reader that this is actually extremely difficult. It puts the brain in a tough situation. Do the long term work and reap the reward? Or settle for the easier short term?

## Template

Most people could [ACHIEVE NORMAL HARD THING] by [DOING OUTSIDE OF THE BOX THINKING HARD THING] every day for a few years.

## Example

Most athletes could go D1 by obsessively studying one elite player's footwork every day for a few years.
