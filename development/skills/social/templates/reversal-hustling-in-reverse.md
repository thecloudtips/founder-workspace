## Why It Works

Everyone talks about 'hustling' so this reverses it on people. What would it be like if hustling was the worst thing in the world? The answer: hustling while neglecting your personal life. Then it uses wordplay by calling it 'hustling in reverse'.

## Template

If your [NormalGoodThing] means [ReallyBadThing], you're [Verb] in reverse.
