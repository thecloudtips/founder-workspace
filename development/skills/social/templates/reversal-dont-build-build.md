## Why It Works

Both of these work for the same reason: Surprise reversal. For example, in the first one, I tell you not to build a business, and then reverse it by simply describing business in a much more pleasing way.
Same with the second one. I take a flex (driving a nice car to work) and find the reversal inside of it (not having to drive to work).

## Template

Don't build a [Thing].
Build a [More pleasing version of a thing]

---

The real flex isn't the [Thing].
It's not having to [Do thing at all].

## Example

Don't build a campaign.
Build a conversation that generates leads.

---

The real flex isn't the ad budget you spend.
It's not needing an ad budget at all.
