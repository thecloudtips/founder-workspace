Fix it by doing these 3 things:
1. Focus on search intent:
- Stop guessing what keywords mean
- Study the top 10 results
- Match your content to what ranks
1. Build content clusters:
- Create deep topic expertise
- Link related content strategically
- Own entire subject areas
1. Track behavior metrics:
- Monitor time on page
- Watch scroll depth
- Study exit patterns

They chase raw traffic numbers.
Wrong.
