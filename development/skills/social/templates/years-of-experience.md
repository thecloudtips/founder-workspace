## Why It Works

The goal was to tap into a widely accepted rule of thumb — that how people treat those who serve them is a window into what kind of person someone is. By opening with "42 years on this earth," the post shares wisdom rather than just an opinion.

## Template

After [X years of experience in a specific field or life in general], I can say with 100% certainty that you shouldn't [engage in behavior A] with people who [display behavior B].
It's a sign of [underlying issue or characteristic] and what your future [relationship or experience] will look like.

*Note: The first part of this Tweet alone would work well on its own.*

## Example

After 20 years of navigating relationships, I can say with 100% certainty that you shouldn't commit to someone who can't apologize.
It's a sign of emotional immaturity and what your future interactions will look like.
