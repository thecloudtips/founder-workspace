## Why It Works

The purpose of this Tweet is to be direct and honest — a hard-hitting truth that can be eye-opening and impactful. It subtly calls people to the carpet by talking about the ease of accessing information today.

## Template

Truth most people need to hear about [Topic or Field]:
You can't be [A Successful Individual in This Field] if you can't solve [Type of Problems] on your own.
Nearly every piece of information on [Topic or Skill] is available with a few keystrokes.
There are literally zero excuses.

## Example

Truth most people need to hear about Fitness:
You can't be fit and healthy if you can't solve basic nutrition and exercise challenges on your own.
Nearly every piece of information on diet plans and workout routines is available with a few keystrokes.
There are literally zero excuses.
