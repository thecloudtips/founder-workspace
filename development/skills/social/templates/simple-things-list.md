## Why It Works

A simple opener catches attention, followed by a simplified description of how to get started. The white space and dashes work well — people on social media aren't looking for encyclopedias of information. Short, clear, simple, and basic beats being overly academic.

## Template

I know people doing [BIG THING] with nothing more than:
- [SIMPLE THING 1]
- [SIMPLE THING 2]
- [SIMPLE THING 3]
- [SIMPLE THING 4]
That's it.
Do yourself a favor and don't overcomplicate.
Start simple — you'll be glad you did.

## Example

I know people doing six-figure businesses with nothing more than:
- A laptop

- A simple website
- One social media account
