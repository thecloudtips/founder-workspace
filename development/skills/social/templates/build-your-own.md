## Why It Works

This template uses empowering language built around the idea that self-built businesses are the best to work for. It lists tangible benefits of entrepreneurship — accountability, skill acquisition, prioritizing family — while acknowledging realistic challenges. The closing "You." lands with personal impact.

## Template

Strongly held belief:
You'll never work for a better [organization/business/project] than the one you build.
When you [take this path]:
1. You're [Type of Responsibility].
2. You learn [Skills or Roles].
3. You choose to [Personal Priority].
Are there hard days? Definitely.
Does it always go according to plan? [Rhetorical Question with Negative Answer].
But, in the end, you're working for the only person who genuinely cares about [Aspect of Your Life].
You.

## Example

Strongly held belief:
You'll never create a better story than the one you write yourself.
When you embark on writing:
1. You're entirely in control of the narrative.
2. You learn every aspect of character and plot development.
3. You choose to prioritize your creative expression.
Are there hard days? Absolutely.
Does it always flow smoothly? Not a chance.
But, in the end, you're crafting a tale for the only person who truly understands the story you want to tell.
You.
