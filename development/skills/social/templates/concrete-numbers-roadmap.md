## Why It Works

This template breaks down success into a simple, achievable formula using real numbers and real goals. It takes an abstract idea and turns it into a concrete daily task. The narrative of small consistent actions multiplying into significant results sparks "What if that were me?" moments. It's a clear one-two-three step process that acts as a starting block, not just advice.

## Template

Reach [specific number] of [daily action or habit], and you're on track for [larger goal or outcome].
Even [small consistent action] can lead to [significant impact or result].
Focus on one key [skill/habit/project], develop it, and [related action].
That's your starting point.

## Example

Connect with just 3 new people in your field each day, and you're building a network that's both broad and deep.
Imagine — 1,095 new friends who can open doors to opportunities and insights.
Create value on the internet, draw people in, and connect.
Start there.
