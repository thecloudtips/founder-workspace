## Why It Works

It worked because of some strong language ('smashes' the myth) and because it's an inspiring story that a ton of solopreneurs can relate to. Being relatable is one way to connect with your content.

## Template

Had a great Zoom with a [Person].
He smashes the myth of '[Myth]'.

- Year 1: [NotGreat, Money]
- Year 2: [SlightlyBetter, Money]
- Year 3: [SlightlyBetter, Money]
- Year 4: [MuchBetter, Money]
- Year 5: [Incredible, Money]

Awesome.
