## Why It Works

I start by knocking down old-school status symbols one by one, then reveal a better path forward. By listing out the fancy house, car, and watch first, you can feel the tension building — right before I flip the script with what actually matters. Then those bullet points hit home because everyone's felt that pain of missing dinners and asking for permission. It makes freedom feel like the obvious next step.

## Template

Your success isn't measured by [STATUS SYMBOL 1].
Or [STATUS SYMBOL 2].
Or [STATUS SYMBOL 3].
Or [STATUS SYMBOL 4].
The most fulfilled [PEOPLE TYPE] I know?
They're building [THING] that serve their lives, not consume them.
[SMALL NUMBER] hours of [POSITIVE DESCRIPTOR] work daily.
The rest? Actually [LIVING DESCRIPTOR].
No more:
- [TRADITIONAL WORK PAIN 1]
- [TRADITIONAL WORK PAIN 2]
- [TRADITIONAL WORK PAIN 3]
- [TRADITIONAL WORK PAIN 4]
Just the freedom to [ASPIRATIONAL ACTION].
Want to build a [THING] that fits your life (and not the other way around)?
[YOUR CALL-TO-ACTION]

## Example

Here's an example using the template, written from the perspective of a marketer.

Your success isn't measured by the size of your email list.
Or the followers you have.
Or the likes you get.
Or the platform you're on.
The most fulfilled marketers I know?
They're building relationships that serve their lives, not consume them.
2-3 hours of strategic work daily.
The rest? Actually creating.
No more:
- Posting 20 times a day
- Chasing viral moments
- Trading content for pennies
- Asking algorithms for permission
Just the freedom to build authentic connections.
Want to build a marketing system that fits your life (and not the other way around)?
[YOUR CALL-TO-ACTION]
