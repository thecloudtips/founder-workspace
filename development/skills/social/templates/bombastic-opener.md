## Why It Works

The thread started with a slightly bombastic statement: "Everyone should have a side project that earns $150+ per day." People will either say "hell yes!" because they know having extra income is a must, or get upset because the statement is provocative. Both responses drive engagement. The format is then simple and repeatable: a header, why it's liked, and a few supporting lines — organized and repeated across the thread.

## Template

Everyone should have [SLIGHTLY BOMBASTIC STATEMENT].
Here are 4 simple examples that might help:

1. [EXAMPLE 1 HEADER]
Why I like [EXAMPLE 1]:
[STATEMENT 1]
[STATEMENT 2]
[STATEMENT 3]
