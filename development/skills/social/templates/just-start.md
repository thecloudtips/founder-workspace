## Why It Works

This template presents a simple, action-oriented message using direct language that is relatable and easy to understand. It contrasts a real-world approach with passive learning, which is something many people struggle with. The contrast between doing and just consuming is immediately relatable.

## Template

If you're thinking about [Pursuing a Goal or Activity], you don't need another [Thing 1], [Thing 2], or [Thing 3].
Just start.
You'll learn 100x more by [Experiencing a Challenge] than by [Passive Activity].

## Example

If you're thinking about getting fit, you don't need another diet plan, workout video, or health blog.
Just start.
You'll learn 100x more by pushing your limits at the gym than by reading someone else's fitness journey.
