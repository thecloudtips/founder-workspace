## Why It Works

This Tweet cuts through the noise with a clear game plan for business, speaks to the dream of outlasting the competition, offers a tangible framework to make it happen, and strikes a chord with the patience it takes to build a vision in a world that's all about instant gratification. All in all, a nice combination.

## Template

Here is how I think about [Topic].
- [Big Picture]: [Longest time frame]
- [Plan]: [Second longest time frame]
- [Actions]: [Shorter time frame]
- [Execution]: [Shortest time frame]
I plan on [unique value proposition or strategy].

## Example

Here is how I think about my fitness:
- Health: Lifetime
- Diet: Monthly
- Exercise: Weekly
- Choices: Daily
I simply focus on making better choices every day.
