## Why It Works

This Tweet likely resonated because it simplifies an issue that most people struggle with into two simple, actionable steps. The goal is to make something complex seem very manageable. It also calls out a common miss seen so often — ignoring data and customer feedback.

## Template

Every problem in [Industry or Topic] can be solved 1 of 2 ways:
1. [Quantitative action]
2. [Qualitative action]
Most people do neither.

## Example

Every problem with weight loss can be solved 1 of 2 ways:
1. Analyzing your caloric intake
2. Talking to your trainer
Most people do neither.
