## Why It Works

I start with five specific examples of protecting time that everyone wishes they did more often.
The structure builds momentum because each bullet point feels both rebellious and responsible at the same time. Getting slightly longer with each line.
Then I close with a punchy reminder about time being valuable — but the real power is that I've just shown you exactly how to protect it.

## Template

I've never regretted:
- [ACTION THAT SAVES TIME 1]
- [ACTION THAT SAVES TIME 2]
- [ACTION THAT SAVES TIME 3]
- [ACTION THAT PROTECTS TIME 4]
- [ACTION THAT INVESTS TIME 5]
Protect your [VALUABLE RESOURCE] like it's your most valuable asset because it is.

## Example

I've never regretted:
- Ending a study session when focus drops
- Saying no to "just one more question"
- Taking brain breaks between sections
- Blocking my calendar for practice tests
- Making time for daily vocabulary review
Protect your mental energy like it's your most valuable asset because it is.
