## Why It Works

This is a combination of visual pleasure (lines getting shorter, easier to read) and an anaphora, which is using the same opener multiple times to build to a crescendo (In your…In your…In your…)

## Template

In your [first stage], [action verb 1].
In your [second stage], [action verb 2].
In your [third stage], [action verb 3].
Most people spend their whole lives [common mistake] and never live any of these stages.

## Example

Athletics 101:
In your youth, explore.
In your teens, specialize.
In your 20s, master.
Most athletes spend their whole lives jumping between activities.
The true cost? Never experiencing the deep satisfaction of mastery.
