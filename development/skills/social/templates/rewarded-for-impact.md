## Why It Works

This template cuts through the noise and directly challenges the notion that hard work alone leads to success. It makes an impact statement right off the bat — the key driver of its effectiveness — then reframes common mistakes toward optimal strategies.

## Template

You aren't rewarded for [Commonly Held Belief].
You're rewarded for [More Effective Approach].
So don't [Common Mistake] when you could [Optimal Strategy]

## Example

You aren't rewarded for spending hours in the gym.
You're rewarded for effective workouts.
So don't exhaust yourself doing random exercises when you could follow a structured fitness plan.
