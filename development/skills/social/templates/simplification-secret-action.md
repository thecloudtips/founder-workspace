## Why It Works

Mostly I'm saying the same thing I always say: Get started. Remember that by saying 1 thing 1,000 different ways, people become familiar with your message. The formatting here is key. Short opening hook, contextual line, and content organized in 3 short bullets, with a takeaway.

## Template

The secret to [DESIRED OUTCOME] is [KEY ACTION].
Don't wait for [IDEAL SCENARIO]. [TRUTH ABOUT IDEAL SCENARIO].
- [ACTIONABLE ADVICE 1]
- [ACTIONABLE ADVICE 2]
- [ACTIONABLE ADVICE 3]
[MOTIVATIONAL STATEMENT]

## Example

Here's an example using the template, written from the perspective of an athlete:

The secret to athletic success is consistency.
Don't wait for the perfect training conditions. They don't exist.
- Start with your current fitness level
- Use the equipment available to you
- Do the workouts you can manage today
Imperfect training beats perfect excuses.
