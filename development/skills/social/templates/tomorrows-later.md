## Why It Works

This template taps into the universal human tendency to procrastinate, making the message relatable to a huge audience. The use of a large number of "tomorrows" paints a vivid picture of the cost of inaction over time. The goal is to be concise yet thought-provoking, making the reader consider their own postponed plans or ambitions.

## Template

People love the idea of [Desired Action or Goal] but always think they'll do it tomorrow.
Then they wake up [Large Number] tomorrows later and realize they never did.

## Example

People love the idea of lifting weights but always think they'll start tomorrow.
Then they wake up 7,000+ tomorrows later and realize they never did.
