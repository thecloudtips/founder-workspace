## Why It Works

This template addresses common myths about a topic and then contrasts them with a better reality. People love compare and contrast content. Using concrete examples and specific details (like working hours, vacation days, or revenue figures) makes the post more credible and engaging.

## Template

Myths of [Topic or Profession]:
- [Common Belief #1]
- [Common Belief #2]
- [Common Belief #3]
My reality:
- [Your Reality #1]
- [Your Reality #2, including a specific metric or example]
- [Your Reality #3, including a specific metric or example]
- [Additional Point if needed]
Don't believe everything you read about [Topic or Profession].

## Example

Myths of Fitness:
- You need to train for hours every day
- You can't enjoy your favorite foods
- Results are only about losing weight
My reality:
- I exercise efficiently for 30 minutes daily
- I enjoyed a slice of pizza last night without guilt
- Gained strength, not just lost weight — lifted 10 lbs more this week
- Feeling more energetic and positive
Don't believe everything you read online about Fitness.
