# Proposal Section Templates

Detailed examples for each of the 7 proposal sections. Use these as structural guides — adapt language, tone, and content to fit each client's specific context.

---

## 1. Cover Letter Template

```markdown
# Proposal: [Project Title]

**Prepared for:** [Client Name]
**Prepared by:** [Provider Name]
**Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD]

---

Dear [Contact First Name],

Following our conversation on [date/context], it's clear that [Client Name] is facing
[specific challenge from brief — e.g., "a growing gap between customer acquisition and
the operational capacity to serve them"]. This proposal outlines a focused approach to
[outcome — e.g., "close that gap with an automated onboarding system that scales with
your growth"].

Our approach centers on [methodology summary — e.g., "building a modular platform in
three phases, starting with the highest-impact workflow automation and expanding to
self-service capabilities"]. The result: [primary value — e.g., "your team reclaims 20+
hours per week currently spent on manual onboarding tasks"].

[If CRM data available:] We've had the pleasure of working with [Client Name] on
[past project], and this proposal builds on that foundation — [specific connection to
past work].

I'd welcome the opportunity to walk through these options together. Please reach out
at [email/phone] to schedule a review call, or reply to confirm which package best
fits your goals.

Best regards,
[Provider Name]
```

**Annotations:**
- Paragraph 1 names the client's problem using their language, not yours
- Paragraph 2 previews the approach without detail — detail lives in later sections
- Paragraph 3 only appears when CRM data provides past project context
- Paragraph 4 closes with an explicit next step — never end with "we look forward to hearing from you"

## 2. Executive Summary Template

```markdown
## Executive Summary

**The Challenge:** [Client Name] currently [problem description in business terms —
e.g., "processes customer onboarding manually, requiring 3 staff members and averaging
5 business days per new account"]. This creates [business impact — e.g., "a bottleneck
that limits growth to 20 new customers per month and risks errors in compliance documentation"].

**The Solution:** This engagement delivers [2-3 key deliverables — e.g., "an automated
onboarding platform, a self-service customer portal, and an integrated compliance
document generator"]. The system [primary mechanism — e.g., "replaces the 12-step
manual process with a 3-step automated workflow triggered by CRM events"].

**The Value:** Upon completion, [Client Name] gains [quantified benefit — e.g.,
"the capacity to onboard 100+ customers per month with the existing team, reducing
per-customer onboarding cost from $150 to under $20"]. [Secondary benefit — e.g.,
"Compliance documentation is generated automatically, eliminating the regulatory risk
of manual errors"].
```

**Annotations:**
- Three paragraphs, three purposes: problem → solution → value
- Quantify wherever possible — hours, dollars, percentages
- A C-suite reader should understand the full engagement from this section alone
- Under 300 words total

## 3. Understanding & Approach Template

```markdown
## Understanding & Approach

### Your Situation

[Client Name] operates in [industry context]. Your current [process/system] handles
[current state], but [pain point 1] and [pain point 2] are creating [business consequence].

Based on our review, three underlying factors drive these challenges:

1. **[Root cause 1]** — [explanation of why this matters]
2. **[Root cause 2]** — [explanation]
3. **[Root cause 3]** — [explanation]

### Our Approach

To address these challenges, this engagement follows a [methodology name — e.g.,
"phased delivery model"]:

**Phase 1: [Name]** — [What happens and why it comes first]
**Phase 2: [Name]** — [What happens and how it builds on Phase 1]
**Phase 3: [Name]** — [What happens and the final state]

This approach fits [Client Name] specifically because [tailored rationale — e.g.,
"your team needs to maintain current operations during the transition, and a phased
rollout minimizes disruption while delivering measurable value at each checkpoint"].
```

**Annotations:**
- Restate the client's situation in original language — never copy-paste from the brief
- Identify root causes the client may not have articulated
- Name the methodology and justify why it fits this client specifically

## 4. Scope of Work Template

```markdown
## Scope of Work

### Deliverables

| Deliverable | Description | Acceptance Criteria | Milestone |
|-------------|-------------|---------------------|-----------|
| Onboarding Platform | Automated customer onboarding system with CRM integration | Processes test accounts through full lifecycle with 0 errors | Phase 1 |
| Customer Portal | Self-service portal for document submission and status tracking | 3 test users complete onboarding without staff assistance | Phase 2 |
| Compliance Engine | Automated compliance document generation from customer data | Generates all 5 required documents matching current templates | Phase 2 |
| Admin Dashboard | Staff interface for monitoring, overrides, and reporting | Displays real-time pipeline status for all active onboardings | Phase 3 |
| Documentation | Technical docs, user guide, and runbook | Covers all features; new team member completes setup using docs alone | Phase 3 |

### Exclusions

| Excluded Item | Rationale |
|---------------|-----------|
| Mobile native app | Web portal is mobile-responsive; native app is a Phase 2 consideration |
| Data migration from legacy system | Requires separate data migration assessment; recommend parallel operation during transition |
| Ongoing maintenance | Post-delivery maintenance available as a separate retainer agreement |
| Third-party API customization | Integration uses published APIs only; custom API work requires vendor engagement |

### Assumptions

- Client provides access to CRM system and API credentials by project kickoff
- Client designates a point of contact available for weekly check-ins
- Feedback on deliverables is provided within 3 business days of submission
- Current compliance document templates are provided in editable format by Week 1
- Requirements are stable after the discovery phase; changes after that trigger Change Control
```

## 5. Timeline & Milestones Template

```markdown
## Timeline & Milestones

| Phase | Deliverables | Weeks | Dependencies |
|-------|-------------|-------|--------------|
| Kickoff & Discovery | Requirements review, environment setup, access provisioning | Week 1 | Signed proposal, API credentials |
| Phase 1: Core Platform | Onboarding Platform | Week 4 | Kickoff complete |
| Phase 2: Self-Service | Customer Portal, Compliance Engine | Week 7 | Phase 1 accepted |
| Phase 3: Operations | Admin Dashboard, Documentation | Week 9 | Phase 2 accepted |
| Final Delivery | Handoff, training session, go-live support | Week 10 | All phases accepted |

**Key dates:**
- **Week 1**: Kickoff call and access provisioning
- **Week 4**: Phase 1 demo and client acceptance
- **Week 7**: Phase 2 demo and client acceptance
- **Week 10**: Final delivery and training session
```

## 6. Pricing Template

```markdown
## Pricing

### Package Comparison

| | Foundation | **Growth ✓** | Transformation |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Scope** | Core platform only | Platform + portal + compliance | Full vision with dashboard |
| **Deliverables** | 2 | 4 | 5 |
| **Timeline** | 5 weeks | 8 weeks | 10 weeks |
| **Support** | 1-week hypercare | 2-week hypercare | 4-week hypercare |
| **Investment** | $15,000 | $28,000 | $42,000 |

_Recommended: Growth package — delivers the highest-impact automation while maintaining a focused scope._

### Foundation Package — $15,000

**Includes:** Onboarding Platform, Documentation
**Timeline:** 5 weeks
**Best for:** Teams that need core automation quickly with a tight budget

### Growth Package — $28,000 ✓ Recommended

**Includes:** Onboarding Platform, Customer Portal, Compliance Engine, Documentation
**Timeline:** 8 weeks
**Best for:** Teams ready to eliminate manual processes and enable customer self-service

### Transformation Package — $42,000

**Includes:** All Growth deliverables + Admin Dashboard + extended hypercare
**Timeline:** 10 weeks
**Best for:** Organizations planning to scale significantly and needing operational visibility
```

**Annotations:**
- Comparison table leads with Scope, not Investment
- Recommended package marked with checkmark in header
- Each package has its own section with explicit inclusions
- "Best for" helps clients self-select without price anchoring

## 7. Terms & Conditions Template

```markdown
## Terms & Conditions

### Payment Terms

Payment is tied to deliverable acceptance, not calendar dates:

| Milestone | Amount | Trigger |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Project kickoff | 30% | Signed proposal |
| Phase 1 acceptance | 30% | Client signs off on Phase 1 deliverables |
| Final delivery | 40% | Client signs off on all remaining deliverables |

### Change Control

Changes to scope, timeline, or deliverables require a written Change Order. The provider
documents the change, its impact on timeline and investment, and presents it for client
approval before work begins. Minor adjustments (under 8 hours of effort with no timeline
impact) may be absorbed at provider discretion.

### Intellectual Property

All custom work product created during this engagement becomes the exclusive property of
[Client Name] upon receipt of final payment. The provider retains the right to reference
the project in anonymized portfolio materials unless the client requests otherwise in writing.

### Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of proprietary information disclosed
during the engagement. This obligation survives the completion of the project for a period
of two years.
```
